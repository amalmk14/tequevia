SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY

1. Shipping

All orders for our products are processed and shipped within 2 business days and assure delivery in 7–9 days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. In case of high order volume, shipments may be delayed. If there will be a significant delay, we will notify you via email.

2. Wrong Address Disclaimer

It is the customer’s responsibility to ensure the shipping address entered is correct. If you realize that your address is incorrect, contact us immediately at updates@jerseyloft.com. We will attempt to correct it if the order has not shipped.

3. Undeliverable Orders

Orders returned to us as undeliverable due to incorrect address are subject to a restocking fee. The fee will depend on the returned items.

4. Lost or Stolen Packages

Jersey Loft is not responsible for lost or stolen packages. If tracking shows delivery but you have not received it, please contact the shipping carrier and local authorities.

5. Return Request Days

Returns are allowed within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unopened, unused, and in original packaging. See our Refund Policy for more.

6. Out-of-Stock Items

If any item is out of stock, we will hold the order until all items are available. For extended delays, we will notify you by email.

7. Import Duty and Taxes

For international orders, import duties and taxes may apply. Jersey Loft offers pre-paid options so taxes and duties are included in the price for a smooth experience.

8. Acceptance of Terms

By using our site and placing an order, you agree to this Shipping Policy.

9. Contact Information

Questions about shipping? Contact us at: updates@jerseyloft.com
